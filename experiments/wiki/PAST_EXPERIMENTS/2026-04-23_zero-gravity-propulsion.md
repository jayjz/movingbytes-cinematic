## Results

### Theoretical Feasibility: PLAUSIBLE but UNPROVEN

**Supported by established physics:**
- ✅ Casimir effect proves vacuum has energy and exerts force (measured in labs)
- ✅ Dynamical Casimir effect generates real photons from vacuum
- ✅ Quantum vacuum fluctuations are real (Lamb shift, spontaneous emission)
- ✅ General relativity allows spacetime engineering in principle

**Major engineering hurdles:**
- ❌ Energy densities required are enormous (Planck-scale)
- ❌ No known method to create "negative energy" at macroscopic scales
- ❌ Asymmetric Casimir forces measured are ~nanoNewtons (insufficient for lift)
- ❌ Metamaterials for vacuum coupling don't exist yet
- ❌ "Vacuum catastrophe" - theoretical vs observed vacuum energy differs by 10^120

### Five Potential Approaches Ranked by Near-Term Feasibility:

1. **Asymmetric Nano-Cavity Arrays (McCulloch QI)**
   - Arrays of 129nm asymmetric cavities
   - Predicted thrust: ~9.8 m/s² from unexcited vacuum
   - Status: Theoretical, no experimental validation
   - Timeline: 20-50 years if physics is correct

2. **High-Frequency EM Field Modulation (Zinsser/Vallee)**
   - Microwave/RF fields in resonant cavities
   - Creates local vacuum polarization
   - Status: Anomalous thrust claims, not reproducible
   - Timeline: Unknown (may be measurement error)

3. **Superconducting Quantum Interference**
   - Use superconductors to modify vacuum coherence
   - Potential for "vacuum laser" effect
   - Status: Purely theoretical
   - Timeline: 50+ years

4. **High-Energy Plasma Confinement**
   - Create false vacuum states via extreme conditions
   - Similar to early universe conditions
   - Status: Requires unattainable energies
   - Timeline: Probably never with current physics

5. **Exotic Matter/ Negative Energy (Alcubierre)**
   - Requires matter with negative mass-energy
   - Violates known energy conditions
   - Status: Mathematically possible, physically dubious
   - Timeline: Physics breakthrough needed
